FRECCA is motivated by one goal, to utterly defeat the practice of gender inequality in developing countries. Specifically, we plan to eradicate the Female Genital Mutilation act.

We believe everyone is entitled to a life without ethnic constraints that leave unbearable scars in their life. We aim to put a smile on the helpless and let them know that life is worth living!

With adequate information to both sexes, we will be able to impact the younger generation from following customs that take us back to stone age.
